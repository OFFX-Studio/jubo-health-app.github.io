# Jubo Viva — Concept & Proposal

**B2C concept · Jubo × AI measurement**

> **Your heartbeat brings a little world to life.**

Meet **Bobo** and its tank — a living aquarium where your real health signals (a face scan and your health data) keep the water clear and every creature thriving. Show up for yourself, and it comes alive.

Jubo Viva is a direct-to-consumer wellness app and a companion to Jubo's B2B platform, powered underneath by Jubo's clinical data and care AI.

---

## The living tank

**Your vitals keep a whole tank alive.** Bobo and all kinds of creatures live in one tank. Your body signals keep the water clear and everyone thriving — a familiar, long-life theme you can raise together with family.

- **Vitals → water.** A scan clears and oxygenates the water; your daily health data keeps it flowing and bright. Healthy you, healthy tank.
- **Collect creatures &amp; scenes.** Bobo blobs, fish, koi, turtles — plus coral, plants and whole underwater scenes — procedurally generated, so the cast and the shop never run out.
- **Raise it together.** Family share one tank; everyone's healthy days keep it sparkling. One glance and you know they're okay.
- **Never dies.** Creatures are immortal companions. Miss a day and the water just clouds gently — a scan always brings it back.

*Why an aquarium: it's a familiar, culturally resonant theme for Asian and elderly users (zero learning curve); koi/turtles carry longevity meaning; watching fish measurably lowers stress and blood pressure — the calm-water metaphor echoes exactly what the AI face scan measures; and it's production-lean (one reusable scene + procedural creatures).*

---

## The engine — real health actions are the nourishment

Every input is something genuinely good for you. Nothing is gated behind waiting or paying — you keep the water clear and your creatures thriving by taking care of yourself.

- **Daily measurement — Core nourishment (Primary).** The 10-second face scan. Your unique, defensible core, so it gives the biggest payout. The reading shapes Bobo's mood: calm, energized, or tired.
- **Activity & sleep — Everyday nourishment (Secondary).** Steps, calories and sleep, read passively from Apple Health, accruing even when the app is closed. Your ordinary day quietly keeps the water flowing.
- **Mood & breathing — Mind nourishment (Tertiary).** A one-tap mood check-in and optional breathing exercise. Gentle and shame-free — a low mood brings the creature's care, never its disappointment.

**Nourishment is substitutable — you don't need all of it.** A good day can come from a scan *or* a walk *or* a mood check-in. No single action becomes a chokepoint — which is exactly what keeps it gentle for elderly and anxious users, and clear of coercive "measure-or-else" design.

### Lighter daily touches

- **Tiny AI companion.** The creature reacts and offers a plain-language nudge. Kept small on purpose — a helper, not a chatbot to pour your heart into.
- **Family check-in.** Tap-only cards — greetings, care, reminders — answered in one tap. No typing, and every exchange feeds both creatures.
- **Read a health article.** A short daily read, curated by Jubo. Adds a touchpoint, real credibility, and a natural content-partnership seam.

A hybrid that keeps Finch's warmth and adds Water Llama's "collect them all" pull — without the loot-box trap.

- **The bond — Bobo grows with you.** One companion you raise continuously. Bobo reflects your real state day to day, remembers your streaks, and deepens the longer you show up. This is the emotional anchor — the reason to come back tomorrow.

*Characters are earned through consistency, not cash. The health data is never held hostage — you're rewarded for care, never punished for a missed day.*

New creatures, outfits and accessories keep shipping: the studio's ongoing output, and the pull that brings players back tomorrow. Earned by showing up — cosmetics only, never pay-to-win.

## A day inside Jubo Viva

Five taps, most days under two minutes. The two required features — a 10-second measurement and clear history — sit at the center; everything else is warmth around them.

1. **Home** — Bobo greets you; streak and today's nourishment.
2. **Scan** — 10-second face scan → an estimated reading (screening-grade, confirm with a cuff).
3. **Trends** — history & trends; weekly and monthly, exportable for your doctor.
4. **Collection** — your tank's growing cast (生物圖鑑).
5. **Family** — tap a card, no typing; each tap is a Heart that brightens both tanks.

## Family check-in — co-raise a tank, care in one tap

The mechanism is a **shared tank**: build your own, or join a family member's or friend's, and raise it together. There's no message box — just a warm predefined card you tap in one touch; the other person taps one back. Easy for an 80-year-old, and every tap quietly feeds both creatures.

A curated deck of ready-made cards:

- **問候 / Greetings** — 早安, 晚安, 想你了, 今天好嗎
- **關心 / Care** — 多喝水, 天冷加衣, 記得吃藥, 量個血壓吧
- **提醒 / Reminders** — 該散步囉, 量測時間到, 記得吃飯
- **一鍵回應 / One-tap replies** — 我很好, 收到, 量好了, 等等就做

**Wired into the game:**

- **Every tap is a Heart.** Sending or answering a card sends a Heart. Both creatures glow and gain a little warmth — connection itself feeds the game.
- **Shared streak.** When a reminder gets a reply the same day ("量測時間到" → "✅ 量好了"), you extend a streak you own together. A missed day never scolds; it just waits.
- **Bloom together.** Family-streak milestones unlock shared cosmetics — a matching habitat or a duo outfit for both Bobos. Earned by staying in touch, never bought.

## Design guardrails — healthy, not manipulative

- **✕ Not Candy Crush.** No engineered frustration. Measurement earns energy — it never gates it. You're never locked out of your own health data to force a return.
- **✕ Not a companion trap.** The AI stays deliberately tiny — a reactive creature, not an attachment-maximizing chatbot. That keeps it clear of the regulatory heat around companion apps.
- **✓ Pikmin-gentle.** Passive accrual and no punishment for lapses. Miss a day and the water clouds gently, then clears — the creatures never die and wait patiently.
- **✓ Wellness, not medical.** AI readings are framed as screening-grade trend-tracking with cuff confirmation — a consumer product, never a diagnostic device.

## System architecture — one app, built as modules

Viva is engineered as independent modules — each a self-contained piece of value that can be built, tested and extended on its own. Clean to develop, easy to grow, and every module is its own long-term stream.

- **Core module (Foundation).** 10-second AI measurement, app lifecycle, and health integration on both platforms — Apple Health and Google Fit. The backbone everything else runs on.
- **Account & profiles.** Sign-in, family linking, subscription entitlements and cross-device sync — who you are, who's in your family, and what's yours.
- **Data visualization.** History, trends and vital charts — turning raw readings into insight at a glance.
- **Simulation — the tank.** The living aquarium: creatures, water health and animation — the emotional heart.
- **Puzzle mini-games.** An extendable slot for bite-size games, each feeding the tank.
- **Interaction.** Add friends and family; shared tanks and tap-cards.
- **Information.** Jubo-curated news and articles — a channel to carry any Jubo concept or message to families.
- **Character assets pipeline.** The art-production system: procedural creatures, outfits and decor — an endless content engine.

*Modular means maintainable and future-proof — each module can grow into new content and new value without touching the rest.*

## The business — a consumer business in its own right

Jubo Viva stands on proven free-to-play wellness economics: a large free base, gentle cosmetic and subscription revenue, and content partnerships. It's built to be a real business. And because it runs on Jubo's data and reaches Jubo's future customers, it does something a standalone app never could — it compounds the B2B core underneath.

### How the app makes money

- **Free core (Acquisition).** Scan, trends, Bobo, check-in — all free. The wide, sticky top of the funnel that every other stream depends on.
- **Cosmetics & habitats (Micro-transactions).** Tank decor, coral, creature outfits, seasonal themes and backgrounds. High-margin, repeatable, self-expression spend — never power, never a loot-box.
- **Viva+ (Subscription).** Recurring revenue from deeper multi-metric trends, doctor-ready exports and a larger family circle.
- **Curated content & brands (Partnerships).** Jubo-vetted articles and programs open a content, insurer and pharma revenue seam.

### Viva+ pays the build back

**US$6.99 / month · US$69.99 / year** (~NT$220 / NT$2,200). Roughly **1,600 yearly Viva+ subscribers recoups the full NT$3M build in year one** — net of the ~15% platform fee. From Jubo's 2,700+ facilities and their families, that's a small conversion, and cosmetics revenue sits on top of this. *(Illustrative; App Store / Play take 15–30%; final Taiwan pricing set at launch.)*

### On top of that — it strengthens Jubo's core

Jubo is B2B: AI care SaaS for **2,700+** facilities, **330,000+** professionals, **3 billion+** care records. A profitable consumer app that also feeds that core is worth far more than its own P&L.

- **Feeds Jubo AI.** Consented AI measurement & wellness signals from healthy, community-dwelling elders *complement* the institutional 3B-record dataset — sharpening N-Copilot, risk prediction, and the whole care-AI edge.
- **Top-of-funnel to B2B.** Viva reaches families *before* institutional care is needed. When it is, Jubo is already the trusted name — feeding the care-facility matching platform and the B2B sales pipeline.
- **Extends the care continuum.** Jubo covers residential, day, and home care. Viva extends coverage upstream to aging-at-home and prevention — so Jubo owns the whole aging journey.
- **Public trust & the equity story.** Extends the FamilyLine family layer to the general public. A recognised consumer brand strengthens B2B sales and the Compal Health internationalization & capital-markets narrative.

**The flywheel back to the core:** more families → signals + trust (consented data · in-network families) → stronger core (smarter AI · warmer B2B funnel) → fuels Viva (credibility & funding back).

## Go to market

The buyer isn't always the user: an adult child often installs and champions it for a parent. The plan leans into that — and into a distribution channel no consumer startup has.

**The unfair channel.** Jubo already reaches 2,700+ facilities, 330,000+ professionals and their residents' families — plus existing family apps like FamilyLine. That's warm, trusted distribution straight to exactly the right households, so blended acquisition cost stays far below a cold-start consumer app.

- **Phase 1 · Beta — Seed & learn.** Staff & families at Jubo facilities; existing Jubo family-app users; instrument retention hard.
- **Phase 2 · Soft launch — Community pull.** ASO + article-driven SEO; wellness & eldercare KOLs; LINE / FB eldercare family groups; built-in family-invite loop.
- **Phase 3 · Scale — Paid & partners.** Paid UA (Meta / Google / LINE); insurer, pharmacy, local-gov eldercare; App Store featuring; TW first, then JP.
- **Phase 4 · Always-on — Live marketing.** Content-drop & seasonal campaigns; CRM & win-back; referral compounding.

**Channels:** Owned (Jubo network + family apps) · Organic (ASO + content) · Community (KOLs + family groups) · Paid & partners (UA + institutions).
